    Résumé (Brief)

    Researcher · Software Developer · Educator, Ph.D.
    Aarhus, Denmark

    Profile

    • Researcher and software architect specialising in digitalisation, digital twins, and cyber-physical systems.
    • Transforms research ideas into scientific results, open-source platforms, prototypes, and industrial collaborations.
    • Collaborates with industry partners and research groups across Denmark, Italy, Germany, Spain, and Norway.
    • Brings more than a decade of mentoring, teaching, and curriculum development in software engineering and computer science.

    Software Development Motto: Agile software development process enhanced with DevSecOps and Agent Engineering.

    Software Development

    I architect, program, and maintain research software for digital twin development and deployment.

    • DTaaS — Digital Twin as a Service: multi-tenant platform for digital twins and IoT.
    • Digital Twin Workflow for Structural Health Monitoring — Reference implementation prototyping monitoring of wind turbine blades.
    • Simulation Bridge — Distributed simulation middleware for digital twins and IoT systems.
    • DIGITbrain — Architecture and Design for Manufacturing as a Service (MaaS) software.
    • Generalized Deduplication — Data compression package for IoT systems.
    • AutolabJS — Auto evaluation tool for C/C++/Java/Python programming projects.

    Research Projects (Selected)

    • SWiM — Sustainable Water-based Cooling in Megacities (Grundfos Foundation, 60MDKK, 4 partners, 2026–30): researcher & software architect for the digital twin platform for district cooling in Asian megacities.
    • Secure4DTaaS — Secure Collaboration on the DTaaS Platform (Thomas B. Thriges Foundation, 3MDKK, 2025–28): software architect & developer; zero-trust secure sharing of digital twin assets across organisations.
    • CP-SENS — Cyber-Physical Sensing for Machinery and Structures (Danish Innovation Foundation, 19.3MDKK, 6 partners, 2023–27): project board member, architect & developer; reference digital twins for structural health monitoring.
    • DIGITbrain (EU H2020 IA, 8M€, 36 partners, 2020–23): architecture & design of a multi-cloud digital twin platform supporting three SME manufacturing consortia.
    • HUBCAP (EU H2020 IA, 8M€, 17 partners, 2020–22): reviewer & prototype developer for distributed sandbox computing and model-driven engineering.
